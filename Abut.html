About Itszu Bakery

Our Story

Itszu Bakery began as a small dream — a cozy kitchen, a curious heart, and a deep love for baking. What started as a hobby of crafting sweet treats for friends and family turned into a passion for spreading joy through food. With each brownie baked and cake carefully decorated, our goal has always been the same: to create simple moments of happiness in every bite.

What Makes Us Special

  • 🍫 Handmade in small batches for quality and freshness
  • 🎁 Perfect for gifts, events, or a little self-love
  • 💜 Custom orders available — because every customer is unique
  • 🌱 We use only the best ingredients, with love in every layer

Meet the Baker

Hi! I'm Izzu — the heart behind Itszu Bakery. I'm a student, a self-taught baker, and a person who finds peace in mixing butter and sugar together. I believe baking is both a science and an art, and I love exploring new flavors, perfecting classics, and turning everyday cravings into special treats.

Our Mission

To bring a little more warmth, sweetness, and human connection into the world through humble homemade desserts. Whether it's a brownie shared between friends or a cake that lights up a birthday, we want to be part of your special moments.

Thank you for supporting our small business. Every order means the world to us. 💕
